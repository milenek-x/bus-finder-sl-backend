About the Awards

The NIBM Best Innovative Business Model Award is an annual competition designed to identify, celebrate, and promote businesses that demonstrate exceptional innovation in their operational models, market strategies, and value creation. Our goal is to foster a culture of innovation and provide a platform for businesses to gain national recognition, attract investment, and inspire others.

We believe that innovative business models are the cornerstone of sustainable growth and economic development. This award aims to highlight those pioneering efforts that are reshaping industries and creating significant positive impact.

Categories & Criteria

The awards are open to businesses across four distinct sectors, each with tailored criteria to ensure fair and relevant assessment.

Start-ups

For emerging businesses (less than 5 years old) showcasing disruptive and scalable innovative business models with significant growth potential and market impact.

  • Novelty of business model
  • Market validation & potential
  • Scalability & sustainability
  • Team strength

MSMEs

Small and Medium Enterprises driving impactful change through innovation in processes, products, services, or market approaches.

  • Innovation in operations/strategy
  • Impact on business performance
  • Adaptability & resilience
  • Contribution to local economy

Large Corporations

For large enterprises that have successfully implemented innovative business models, driving significant transformation or creating new market segments.

  • Strategic innovation
  • Market leadership & disruption
  • Organizational impact
  • Sustainability initiatives

Public Enterprises

For government-owned entities that have introduced innovative models to improve public service delivery or operational efficiency.

  • Public value creation
  • Efficiency & effectiveness
  • Citizen engagement
  • Scalability of impact

Frequently Asked Questions

The awards are open to businesses across four distinct categories: Start-ups, MSMEs, Large Corporations, and Public Enterprises. Specific eligibility criteria are detailed in the 'Categories & Criteria' section.

The application deadline for NIBM Best Innovative Business Model Award 2025 is 30 September 2025. Please refer to the 'Key Dates & Timeline' section for all important dates.

Currently, there are no fees for submission. However, this may be subject to change in future editions. Any updates will be clearly communicated on the website.

Entries will be evaluated by our esteemed Assessment Council based on innovation, impact, scalability, and sustainability. The judging process involves multiple rounds of assessment. More details on the judging criteria are available in the 'Categories & Criteria' section.
